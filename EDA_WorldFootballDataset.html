While Arteta’s xG seems lower than wenger’s, it’s important to note he joined the club during a turbulent period and is first couple of years were spent rebuilding the club. A more accurate comparison would be to calculate Arteta’s average xG until 2022 and his average xG after, which I’ll do now.

arteta_initial_arsenal_xg <- arsenal_xg %>%
                               filter(Date >= as.Date("2019-12-26") & Date <= as.Date("2022-01-01")) %>%
